# ReactionGIF — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-28_

ReactionGIF helps you find a reaction GIF for a conversation. This policy explains
what the app does with your data. Host this text at a public URL and put that URL
in `AppConfig.privacyPolicyURL` and in your App Store Connect listing.

## What we process

- **The screenshot/photo you choose.** When you pick or capture an image, it is
  sent to OpenAI to determine the best reaction. We do not store your images on
  our servers, and we do not use them to build profiles or train models.
- **Search terms.** Short text queries derived from the image are sent to Tenor
  (Google) and Giphy to fetch matching GIFs.
- **Reported GIFs.** If you report a GIF, its URL is stored on your device so we
  never show it to you again (and optionally sent to our server to improve
  filtering).

## What we don't do

- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.
- We do **not** sell or share your personal data.
- We do **not** store your conversations or images on our backend.

## Data handled by third parties

Images and queries are processed by:

- **OpenAI** — https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
- **Tenor (Google)** — https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Giphy** — https://support.giphy.com/hc/en-us/articles/360032872931-GIPHY-Privacy-Policy

## On-device storage

Your selected vibe, "safe mode" setting, recently found GIFs, and reported GIFs
are stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time.

## Children

ReactionGIF is not directed at children under 13.
