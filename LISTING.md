## Description

You know the feeling: the perfect moment in the group chat, and you're *frantically*
searching for the right GIF while everyone moves on. ReactionGIF fixes that.

Just screenshot the conversation. ReactionGIF actually reads it — the last message,
the running joke, the energy — and finds two reactions that land, pulled from the
best of Tenor and Giphy.

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT
• It reads the room. This isn't a search box — the AI understands the conversation
  and picks reactions that fit the moment.
• Pick your energy. Wholesome, Unhinged, Deadpan, Hype, or Petty — set the vibe
  and the picks change to match.
• Two options, instantly. Shuffle for alternatives without starting over.
• One-tap send. Copy or share straight into iMessage, WhatsApp, Instagram, anywhere.
• Paste & go. Got a screenshot on your clipboard? One tap and you're done.

BUILT TO BE SAFE
• Safe Mode forces strictly family-friendly results.
• Report any GIF and you'll never see it again.

No account. No tracking. Just the right reaction, right when you need it.

## Review notes (paste into "Notes for Reviewer")
ReactionGIF sends the user-selected screenshot to OpenAI to determine a fitting
reaction, then fetches GIFs from Tenor and Giphy. Content is filtered (Tenor
contentfilter + Giphy rating), a Safe Mode forces G-rated results, and users can
report any GIF to permanently hide it. No login required.
